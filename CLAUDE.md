# StoreProof

## Product

StoreProof is an AI-powered theft investigation tool for retail staff.

## Core Workflow

Staff logs a theft at a specific time and site → system automatically pulls relevant camera footage → AI filters to cameras where someone was present → AI adds basic notes (e.g. "entered store at 14:32, picked up item, left at 14:45") with a clear warning that AI analysis can be wrong → clip is downloaded automatically → staff can review the footage alongside the AI-generated notes (with a prominent disclaimer before watching that AI can be wrong) → staff makes a police report or takes action.

## Key Principle

AI is a tool to assist staff, not replace human judgement. Every AI-generated note or clip must be accompanied by a clear disclaimer that the analysis may be inaccurate.
